§ 01

Program Overview

The BMPFast Learning System is a comprehensive professional development program for municipal stormwater managers, civil and environmental engineers, regulators, and public works decision-makers. It was built by industry leaders with over 150 years of combined experience in water quality science, BMP design and evaluation, software engineering, and regulatory compliance.

Their combined work has shaped how stormwater professionals across the United States approach permitting, treatment design, and environmental protection — and this program packages that expertise into an accessible, self-paced curriculum that integrates regulatory guidance with hands-on software training.

§ 03

Why Are You Here?

Whether you are a seasoned stormwater professional seeking to stay current or a newer practitioner building your competency, this training program is designed to address the most pressing needs in today's regulatory environment. The BMPFast Learning System was created in direct response to evolving rules, regulations, and best practices that affect how stormwater projects are permitted, designed, and managed across Florida and beyond.

1

Deepen Your Understanding

Obtain more comprehensive understanding and training for stormwater management as it relates to water quality control. The learning modules cover fundamental principles through advanced analytical methods, ensuring that every participant gains the knowledge needed to make informed decisions in the field.

2

Stay Current with Regulations

Collect critical information on the new rules, regulations, and practices affecting Environmental Resource Permits (ERPs) and Basin Management Action Plans (BMAPs). Regulatory landscapes are shifting, and this program ensures you understand what those changes mean for your projects and compliance obligations.

3

Accelerate Permitting

Address how to obtain an ERP permit as fast as possible while ensuring full consistency with new rules, regulations, and current practice. Time-to-permit is a real cost driver for projects, and understanding the process thoroughly reduces delays and costly revisions.

4

Master BMPFast Software

Understand the BMPFast software platform and its capabilities for analyzing stormwater Best Management Practices. The software streamlines complex calculations and supports defensible permit applications with data-driven analysis.

5

Earn a Professional Certificate

Earn a certificate of competency after completing a comprehensive examination. This credential demonstrates proficiency in using stormwater BMPs for water quality control and positions you as a qualified practitioner in your field.

§ 04

Who Is This For?

The BMPFast Learning System is designed for a broad audience of professionals invested in stormwater management — from those just beginning to engage with water quality issues to experienced practitioners refining their skills with the latest tools and regulatory guidance. Participants typically span municipal government, consulting firms, regulatory agencies, and academic institutions and can be grouped into four key categories.

Building Core Competency

Professionals with some foundational knowledge of stormwater principles who want to become fully competent in understanding water quality issues related to stormwater management. This group includes newer engineers, planners, and public works staff who need a solid grounding in BMP science and regulatory frameworks.

Permit Applicants

Individuals who need to complete Environmental Resource Permit (ERP), Basin Management Action Plan (BMAP), and other permit applications. These professionals benefit from understanding how the software and learning modules directly support the documentation and analysis required for successful permit submissions.

Software Developers and Power Users

Professionals who have written or worked with past stormwater software tools — including BMPTrains, OPSEW, SWIMM, SMADA, RETEN, LEASTS, LOADER, PAVER, and others — or software programmers who want to understand the analytical engines behind modern stormwater modeling. This group will appreciate the technical depth of the BMPFast platform.

Regulatory and Legal Professionals

Those who wish to obtain a more thorough understanding of stormwater laws (including foreign IP considerations), regulations such as the Applicant's Handbooks, and evolving practice standards. Staying ahead of regulatory changes is essential for compliance officers, attorneys, and agency reviewers.

The BMPFast Learning System is organized into a comprehensive set of learning modules covering the full spectrum of stormwater management topics — from foundational principles and regulatory history to advanced BMP analysis and treatment train design. Each module is self-contained and can be viewed in any order, allowing participants to customize their learning path based on professional need and experience level.

The initial set of 16 core modules forms the backbone of the training program, providing the knowledge needed to confidently navigate the ERP permitting process. Topics include:

  • Stormwater history and permitting
  • New laws and regulations
  • Rainfall and site characteristics
  • Runoff generation and loading calculations
  • Retention analysis

  • Competency test preparation

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) submitted by registered users serve as the primary guide for determining learning module titles and content. Your questions shape the curriculum.

§ 07

The BMPFast Learning System

BMPFast is a comprehensive professional development platform that integrates education with hands-on analytical tools. It was designed from the ground up to serve stormwater professionals who need both conceptual understanding and practical software capabilities to navigate today's complex permitting landscape. The system bridges the gap between textbook knowledge and real-world application, giving users the confidence to design, analyze, and defend their stormwater management strategies.

The acronym BMPFast stands for BMPs For Assessment of Stormwater Treatment.

📚

Learning Modules

A comprehensive library of educational modules covering water quality issues in stormwater management. Topics range from rainfall characteristics and runoff generation to advanced BMP analysis and treatment train design. Each module is self-paced and may be revisited as many times as needed.

💻

BMPFast Software

A powerful software package designed for the analysis of stormwater Best Management Practices used to meet state water quality standards. BMPFast enables users to model, evaluate, and optimize BMP configurations with precision and efficiency. The software supports ERP permit applications and BMAP compliance documentation.

🎓

Competency Certification

Each participant has the opportunity to earn a Certificate of Competency after completing a comprehensive examination. This certification validates proficiency in using stormwater BMPs for water quality control and demonstrates to employers, clients, and regulatory agencies that the practitioner possesses the required knowledge and skills.

§ 08

Registration for the Stormwater Learning Modules and BMPFast software provides comprehensive access to the full suite of training resources, software tools, and certification pathways. The registration is processed through Daytona State College (DSC) Continuing Education. Upon completion, you will receive your credentials and access instructions via email.

Registration includes the following:

  • Access to all current and future learning modules for the duration of your registration period
  • A unique software registration code that unlocks all features of the BMPFast platform
